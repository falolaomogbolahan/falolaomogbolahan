White Space and Clean Design

The NetNaija

Netnaija.com

The netnaija website makes use of the priciple of white space and clean deign in their site, the spaces left out between graphics,visuals or columns are about the same amount and this gives the website a clean look,the use of white space in this site makes it easy to focus the eyes on the main messages inthe website.

PARC: Repetiton

IBM Nigeria

IBM.Com

In this Ibm website the principle of repetion was made use of throughout the page, this helped to create a sense of cohensive replationship between the elements on the page, repetion used on the page also helps create a sense of movement for viewers and it also strengthend and unified the visual appel of the page.

PARC: Proximity

GT Bank

Gtbank.com

The priciple of proximity was used in this website, elements that are related are placed closely so has to show the relationship and connection between them. a the site is a bank site, important informations are placed at provimity so as to show their importance and also allow the reader or viewer find all this informations easily.
